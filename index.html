Responive Design

is a web design approach that ensures a website or web application looks and functions well across a wide range of devices and screen sizes, from desktop monitors to smartphones.

Why it important

Responsive design is important for several reasons, especially as the way users access the internet continues to evolve.

What are media querries

Media queries are a powerful feature in CSS that allow you to apply different styles based on the characteristics of the device or viewport
